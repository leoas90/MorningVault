# GDPR Rights Addendum

For users in the European Economic Area.

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of any personal data we hold (none — all data stays on-device)
- **Rectification**: Correct any inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data (MorningVault stores minimal data; all cached data can be cleared in Settings)
- **Restriction**: Request we restrict processing
- **Portability**: Request data in a machine-readable format
- **Object**: Object to processing of your data

## Our Position

MorningVault processes no personal data on our servers. All health, calendar, and usage data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us. We have no way to identify individual users or access their briefing data.

## Data We Hold

- **On-device cache**: Weather, market, and RSS data cached locally with 30-minute TTL — cleared when you uninstall the app or clear cache in Settings
- **User preferences**: Theme selection, enabled data sources, tracked symbols — stored in UserDefaults on your device

We do not operate user accounts, analytics, or telemetry.
