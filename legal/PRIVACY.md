# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

MorningVault is designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

MorningVault processes the following data locally on your device:
- **Health data** (Apple HealthKit): Sleep, steps, heart rate variability — never transmitted externally
- **Calendar events**: Today's events only — never stored beyond the briefing session
- **Approximate location**: City-level only, used solely to fetch local weather — never shared with any third party
- **Market data**: Stock/crypto symbols and prices fetched from public APIs — no user identifiers

We do **not** collect, store, or transmit:
- Email addresses
- Precise GPS coordinates
- Device identifiers linked to health or calendar data
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Any data to advertising networks

## How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively to generate your morning briefing. Health and calendar data is processed on-device using Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models). Weather and market data is fetched from public APIs and cached locally with a 30-minute TTL.

## Third-Party Services

- **Weather**: Weather data is fetched from wttr.in using your approximate city name only
- **Market data**: Stock and crypto prices are fetched from CoinGecko and Yahoo Finance — no account or login required
- **RSS feeds**: News headlines are fetched from hardcoded public feeds you choose to enable

## Your Rights

- You can disable any data source at any time in Settings
- "Local-Only Mode" disables all network calls — briefing uses only cached data
- HealthKit and Calendar permissions can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings

## Children

MorningVault is not intended for users under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
